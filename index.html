About Chrono Code LLC

At Chrono Code LLC, we are passionate about transforming everyday challenges into seamless digital experiences. As a forward-thinking software development company, we specialize in creating intuitive iPhone applications that enhance productivity and enrich the lives of our users. Whether you're planning for your financial future, managing your pet’s health, or tracking daily expenses, our apps are built to deliver powerful functionality in a user-friendly design.

Our goal is simple: to make life easier, one app at a time.

Our Apps

Plan to Retire

Take control of your financial future. Plan to Retire is your personal financial planner, helping you map out your retirement with confidence. With simple input options, you can calculate the potential return on investment for your retirement savings based on contribution rates, growth projections, and more. Secure your future today, effortlessly.

Features:

  • Customizable savings goals
  • Easy-to-understand investment growth projections
  • Visualized financial timelines

Clear Costs

Master your budget, effortlessly. Clear Costs is your go-to app for tracking expenses and managing your personal finances. Whether you’re working towards savings goals or simply want to get a better handle on your daily spending, Clear Costs gives you the insights you need to optimize your financial health.

Features:

  • Intuitive expense tracking
  • Custom categories for personalized budgeting
  • Detailed spending insights with visual charts

Watch Fido

Because your pet deserves the best care. Watch Fido helps you stay on top of your pet's health and wellness. From managing vet appointments to tracking medications and vaccinations, this app keeps everything you need in one place so you can focus on keeping your pet happy and healthy.

Features:

  • Appointment and medication reminders
  • Vaccination tracking
  • Personalized pet health records
